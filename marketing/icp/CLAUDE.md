# marketing/icp/ — your ICP research

This folder holds your **ICP research**: who you sell to, their pain points, anti-ICP, voice-of-customer.

**To populate:** run `/icp-research` against your company's website + win-loss data (if you have it).

**For the format:** see [`../../pulse-analytics-example/icp/ICP.md`](../../pulse-analytics-example/icp/ICP.md) — fully populated example for the fictional PulseAnalytics B2B SaaS analytics platform.
