# marketing/brand/ — your brand kit + voice

This folder holds your **brand identity** in two files: `brand-kit.md` (design tokens — colors, typography, spacing) and `brand-voice.md` (voice rules with violation patterns + fix templates).

**To populate:** run `/brand-kit` for design tokens, then `/tov-guidelines` for voice rules.

**For the format:** see [`../../pulse-analytics-example/brand/`](../../pulse-analytics-example/brand/).
