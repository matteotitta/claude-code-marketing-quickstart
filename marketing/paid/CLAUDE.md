# marketing/paid/ — your paid lane

**Paid**: Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, paid social.

This lane follows the same arc as every execution lane: **research** → **strategy** → **execution**.

- [`research/`](./research/) — audit the current state, find gaps
- [`strategy/`](./strategy/) — plan, calendar, decide what to ship
- [`execution/`](./execution/) — the actual artifacts you ship

**Skills that produce content here:** `/paid-campaign-strategy` (channel planning), `/paid-ads-copy` (creative).

**For the format:** see [`../../pulse-analytics-example/paid/`](../../pulse-analytics-example/paid/) — same structure, populated with PulseAnalytics examples.
