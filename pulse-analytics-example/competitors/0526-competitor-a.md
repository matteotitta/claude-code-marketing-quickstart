## Executive summary

The Bizible-style attribution category is PulseAnalytics's most direct competitive set. Their core strength is depth on multi-touch attribution; their core weakness is implementation time + interpretation burden. Threat level: **PRIMARY**. Where they win: enterprise teams with a data analyst in-seat who can interpret raw attribution data. Where PulseAnalytics wins: growth-stage teams (3-12 marketers, no data analyst) who need answers not dashboards.
