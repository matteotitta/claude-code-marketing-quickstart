# marketing/competitors/ — competitor research + aggregate canonical

Holds **per-competitor research files** (dated, e.g., `0526-competitor-a.md`) and the **aggregate canonical** (e.g., `aggregate.md`) — the threat matrix every downstream skill reads.

## What's in here

| File | Status | Produced by |
|---|---|---|
| [`0526-competitor-a.md`](./0526-competitor-a.md) | EXAMPLE — PulseAnalytics's "Bizible-style" competitor; replace in this repo | `/competitor-research` |

In W1.3 you run `/competitor-research` on competitors B and C → adds two more dated files. Then `/competitor-aggregate` synthesizes the three into a single locked `aggregate.md`.

## What downstream skills read this

- `/positioning` — needs competitor anchors + differentiation gaps
- `/product-messaging` — needs status-quo-alternatives section
- `/landing-page-copy` — needs comparison content
- `/sales-enablement` — needs battlecard inputs

## Refresh cadence

Quarterly minimum; fortnightly if you're in a fast-moving AI-native category. Trigger sooner if a competitor makes a major move (funding, launch, repositioning).

## Owner

VP Marketing or Product Marketing lead.
